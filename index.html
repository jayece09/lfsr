What is an LFSR?

A linear feedback shift register is a shift register of which its input is a linear function of its previous state.

The initial value of the LFSR is called its seed, and the stream of values produced by the register is deterministic, as it depends only on the register's previous state.

LFSRs are most commonly used to build pseudo-random number generators.
